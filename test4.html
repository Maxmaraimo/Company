Dance 'Till Dawn

Raspoutine is the felted representation of cabaret. Behind its carefully guarded doors, Raspoutine exudes a scintillating energy. Embracing a ‘dance ’till dawn’ philosophy, the opulent theatrical setting transforms into a vibrant club, sensual and torrid, welcoming those who seek a pulsating sound without moderation. With a focus on decadent sophistication, evenings (and early mornings) spent at Raspoutine are bound to leave a lasting impression.
