Redefining Nocturnal Excellence

Raspoutine Dubai, the city’s most prestigious nightlife destination, is poised to redefine the very essence of next-level nocturnal experiences. Immerse yourself in an extraordinary blend of seduction and sophistication, creating a truly distinctive environment that ensures a night you’ll never forget. Raspoutine consistently demonstrates its dedication to musical excellence by showcasing a roster of exceptional deep house and electronic DJs.

RASPOUTINE DUBAI’S RESIDENT DJ

Dedicated to music from an early age, Paul Blondet initiated his DJ career in 2013 in Lyon, France. Paul’s remarkable talent and innate ability to connect with his audience didn’t go unnoticed, resulting in a flurry of offers from other prominent clubs. Since October 2022, he has held the esteemed position of resident DJ at the legendary Raspoutine club in Dubai, where Paul takes center stage from Tuesday to Friday.

Special Guests

Alongside Raspoutine Dubai’s Resident DJ, we welcome a new guest DJ every week, ensuring a dynamic and ever-evolving musical journey. Together, they create an electric atmosphere that keeps our guests coming back for more, making each visit to Raspoutine a truly memorable experience.
