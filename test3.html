REVOLUTIONIZING DUBAI’S NIGHTLIFE EXPERIENCE

Expanding from its Los Angeles location, the City of Gold has now welcomed the renowned club from the City of Light: a destination where fine dining and high-end entertainment seamlessly blend to craft an extraordinary experience. Once again embracing the universal ‘dance ’till dawn’ motto, Raspoutine Dubai beckons its exclusive clientele to DIFC. The metamorphosis from a refined dinner party into an electrifying club atmosphere is what makes Raspoutine Dubai truly memorable.
